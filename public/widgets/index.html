wGrid Widget

How to Embed?

<iframe src="https://wilburwilliams.uk/widgets/wGrid.html" height="300"></iframe>

Then, you have to specify a query URL parameter based on the theme. ?theme=dark or ?theme=light

So, for example, the whole URL could be https://wilburwilliams.uk/widgets/wGrid.html?theme=dark

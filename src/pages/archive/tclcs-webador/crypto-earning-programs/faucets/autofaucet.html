Autofaucet

Autofaucet(.org)  is a decent faucet that supports LOADS of payout coins, from BTC and ETH, to USDC and MATIC.

How it works

1. Earn FCT

Autofaucet's in-faucet token is called FCT. You earn these tokens via shortlinks, mining (they even have a downloadable miner however I do not recommend mining with autofaucet) or offerwalls. 

2. Exchange

Through autoclaiming you turn FCT into cryptocurrencies, which can be put on your balance or withdrawn directly to faucetpay.

3. Faucetpay withdrawing

Withdraw from autofaucet into faucetpay. Once you have reached the minimum withdrawal, you can withdraw from faucetpay and enjoy your coins! If you do not have faucetpay, all links are at the bottom of the page.

3. Direct withdrawing

For direct withdrawing, wait until you have reached the minimum withdrawal, then withdraw. It might be worth to wait a little longer depending on your coin's tx fees.

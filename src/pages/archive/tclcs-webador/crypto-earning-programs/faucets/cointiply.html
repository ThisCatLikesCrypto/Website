Cointiply

Cointiply is my favourite faucet. It pays in BTC, LTC, DASH and DOGE. It's main feature is the hourly faucet, where you can earn anywhere from 22 coins ($0.0022) to whatever the jackpot is, (usually around 200,000 coins/$20) with about a 0.001% chance.

PTC ads

It has things called PTC ads, a bit like shortlinks but you just click the link, wait an amount of time, complete a captcha, and you are rewarded.

Offerwalls

Like (pretty much) every other faucet site, it has offerwalls where you get paid to sign up for something, play a game, etc.

Mobile ads

On their mobile app, they have an option which allows you to watch mobile ads and earn coins.

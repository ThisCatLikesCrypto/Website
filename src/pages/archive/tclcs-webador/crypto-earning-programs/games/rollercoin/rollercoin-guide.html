Guide

So, you've been convinced to join Rollercoin and want to start. Well, here's the guide for you!

How to start

First, you can click this link and go through the sign-up process. (You probably don't need being told how to do that!)

Next, you will want to click the shop icon, go to event pass, and claim your free event pass. Once that's done, you're ready to go! 

Quests

If you navigate to the event pass tab from earlier, and click on 'event quests', Rollercoin will present you with a list of things you can do every day.

Games

Don't want to deposit? Play games! Some are easier than others, and some yield more hashrate. I recommend finding a balance between this and playing those games.

Depositing

Want to start out with some miners? No problem! You can deposit via BTC, ETH, DOGE, or BNB. MATIC will be added in Season 4. I recommend buying from 'Sales and popular' in the shop, the 'El monstro' or 'Chupracabra' miners from the miners section in the shop, or whatever lootbox is available (not the 500 RLT case one!).

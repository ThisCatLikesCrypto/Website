Rollercoin

 

Rollercoin is a Play-2-Earn virtual crypto mining simulator.

It allows anyone to earn free crypto (As of Season 3, BTC, ETH, RLT, BNB, MATIC, DOGE)

All the coins have their own 'network power' which is determined by the hashrate of all the miners mining that coin, and their mining percentages. All of the coins have a fixed block reward, which may be increased in the future as more players join Rollercoin, or during the x2 events.

How can I get hashrate?

You get hashrate by playing games, through bonus power, and from having miners. Miners can be bought from the shop, bought through lootboxes, gotten through game drops and leaderboard airdrops, and can be obtained from the event pass.

What's the event pass?

The event pass works like a battle pass, used in many games. There is a list of rewards, some which are free, and you can complete quests to unlock them. Most rewards require you to have purchased the 'Upgrade' event pass to get, however. You can also bypass the quests by getting the 'premium' event pass, although this costs a lot more. There is a new event pass every season.

What's that 'RLT' coin you mentioned earlier?

RLT (or Rollertoken) is Rollercoin's in-game cryptocurrency. It is used to purchase miners, the event pass, and lots more. It will also be used on the Marketplace when it launches in Season 4, alongside BTC and ETH.

Marketplace?

Yes, an in-game marketplace! You will be able to purchase miners from events and game drops (parts, used to upgrade miners) from the marketplace with BTC, ETH or RLT.

How much does it pay?

Unless you have a large account, you alone will not be able to earn much, maybe a few dollars a month. However, some of the top miners earn 200+ dollars a month.
